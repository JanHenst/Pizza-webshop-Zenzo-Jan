How to get a good deal

Learn how to order a pizza on the official Pizza Bois website.

Step 1

Go to our Order page.

If you scroll to the top of the page you can see the Order link which you can click on.

Step 2

Choose a category.

On the Order page you can choose which category you want by clicking on one of the many categories we provide.

Step 3

Choose your favorite pizza.

When you click on the category you want you can see a list of pizza of which you can pick your favorite.

Step 4

Open your cart.

On the right side on your screen there is a Show order bar on which you can click.

Step 5

Press the order button.

After you clicked on the bar to open your cart you can see all the pizza's you picked en how much they cost. On the bottom you can see a button which brings you to the payment page.

Step 6

Follow the instructions.

Follow the instructions on the screen by filling in your payment information and confirming your order.

How to get a better deal

Learn how to order a pizza and save money on the official Pizza Bois website.

Step 1

Go to our Account page.

If you scroll to the top of the page you can see the Account link which you can click on.

Step 2

Create your account.

When you are on the Login page you can see on the bottom a link to go to the Sign up page to create your account by filling in your information.

Step 3

Go to our Subscription page.

If you scroll to the top of the page you can see the Subscription link which you can click on.

Step 4

Choose a subscription.

There are four subscription you can see on the Subscription page. You can choose which of the four you want by clicking on one of them.

Step 5

Press the order button.

After having selected a subscription of your choosing, you will be redirected to the information page of that subscription. On this page there is a order button which will bring you to the Payment page after being clicked on.

Step 6

Follow the instructions.

Follow the instructions on the screen by filling in your payment information, your account information and confirming your order.
